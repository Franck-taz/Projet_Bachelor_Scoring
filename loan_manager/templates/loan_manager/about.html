Our Mission

Making Credit Fair and Accessible for Everyone

At Horizon Bank, we believe that access to credit should be based on facts, not bias. Our platform combines advanced data analysis with the irreplaceable judgment of human analysts to ensure every application receives a fair, transparent, and explainable decision.

We are fully committed to the principles of the GDPR — particularly Article 22 — which guarantees that no decision affecting a person is made solely by an automated system. Every loan decision at Horizon Bank is validated by a certified risk analyst.

"Technology should serve people, not replace them. At Horizon Bank, every algorithm has a human behind it."

— Thomas Mercier, Founder & CEO
