Privacy Policy

Horizon Bank SAS, as data controller, is committed to protecting the personal data of its users in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — Regulation (EU) 2016/679 — and the French Data Protection Act (Loi Informatique et Libertés).

Data We Collect

When you create an account or submit a loan application, we collect the following data:

  • Identity data: first name, last name, email address
  • Professional data: age, income, professional experience, job category
  • Housing data: house ownership status, years at current address
  • Asset data: car ownership
  • Geographic data: region of residence
  • Supporting documents uploaded voluntarily (pay slips, rent receipts, work contracts)

⚠ We do not collect any sensitive data as defined by Article 9 of the GDPR — including racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, or sexual orientation. Our scoring model explicitly excludes all discriminatory variables.

Purpose of Processing

Your data is collected and processed for the following purposes:

  • Managing your user account and authenticating your identity
  • Processing your loan application and communicating decisions
  • Supporting our risk analysts in their review process
  • Complying with our legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improving the security and reliability of our platform

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to manage your loan application
  • Legal obligation — compliance with banking and anti-money laundering regulations
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention and platform security

Data Retention

  • Account data: retained for the duration of your account + 3 years after closure
  • Loan application data: retained for 5 years from the date of the decision
  • Supporting documents: retained for 5 years in accordance with banking regulations

Data Recipients

Your personal data is accessible only to Horizon Bank staff with a legitimate need to know — specifically our certified risk analysts. We do not sell, rent, or transfer your data to any third party for commercial purposes.

Section 3 — GDPR Article 22

Automated Processing & Human Oversight

✓ In accordance with Article 22 of the GDPR, Horizon Bank guarantees that no decision significantly affecting you is made solely on the basis of automated processing. Every loan decision is reviewed and validated by a certified human analyst.

How Our Risk Analysis Works

Horizon Bank uses an internal risk analysis tool based on machine learning to assist our analysts in evaluating loan applications. This tool produces a risk assessment based on the financial and professional information you provide.

This automated analysis is strictly an internal decision-support tool. It does not constitute a final decision and is never communicated to applicants. The final lending decision is always made by a certified Horizon Bank risk analyst, who may override or disregard the automated assessment at their discretion.

Your Right to Human Review

Under Article 22 of the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request that your application be reviewed by a human analyst
  • Express your point of view regarding any decision made about your application
  • Contest any decision that you believe does not reflect your actual situation

To exercise these rights, contact us at: dpo@horizonbank.com

Your Rights Under the GDPR

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access (Article 15)

You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you at any time.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations under banking regulations.

Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)

You may request that we temporarily restrict the processing of your data.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You may request your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object (Article 21)

You may object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interest.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer:
Email: dpo@horizonbank.com
Address: Horizon Bank SAS — DPO, 15 Rue de la Paix, 75002 Paris, France

We will respond to your request within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority: CNIL — www.cnil.fr
